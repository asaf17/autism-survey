Instructions

Before beginning the examination you will be asked to asked to provide important information like the child's name, age, and sex. Make sure to fill this portion of the examination correctly and to the best of your ability. All responses will be treated confidentially in accordance with HIPAA regulations.

In order to better understand you, the child you are rating, and your family’s quality of life, please read each question carefully and select one of the provided answers. Select only one response for each item. Please select an answer for each item, even if you are not completely sure of your answer.

There are no correct or incorrect answers. Your responses will be treated confidentially. Some questions are framed negatively and some positively. When a question refers to a child’s “difficulties” this could refer to a specific developmental problem (if present), a set of symptoms or behavioral issues, or simply to everyday life hassles and problems. Please read all questions carefully before responding to be sure you are answering the question accurately.

Once all questions on the page are answered click next to move on to the next page. Once all pages have been filled in, make sure to check all of your answers before clicking submit.
